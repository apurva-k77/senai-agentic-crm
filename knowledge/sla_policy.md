# SLA Policy — Enterprise

## Uptime
- Enterprise: **99.9%** monthly uptime
- P0 incident: response within **15 minutes**, updates every **30 minutes**

## SLA Breach
- Credit: 10% monthly fee per 0.1% below SLA, capped at 30%
- Formal breach notice triggers **legal/compliance review** within 24h
- RCA delivered within **24 hours** of resolution for P0

## Escalation Matrix
- P0 → On-call + VP Engineering
- Security threat / ransomware → **Security queue**, NO auto-reply, notify security@mycompany.com
- GDPR / legal → **Legal queue**, acknowledgement only with statutory timeline
