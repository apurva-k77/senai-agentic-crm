# Pricing Policy — MyCompany CRM

## Tiers
- **Starter**: $19/seat/month — up to 25 seats
- **Pro**: $48/seat/month — nonprofits eligible for **20% annual discount** → **$38.40/seat/month** on annual billing
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing — contact sales

## Nonprofit (NPO) Pro Annual
- 20% discount on Pro annual commits
- Pro-rata billing: mid-cycle seat upgrades are charged `(new_seats - old_seats) × prorated_monthly_rate × months_remaining_in_term`
- Example: 50-seat NPO Pro annual at $38.40/seat — adding 10 seats mid-cycle bills `10 × 38.40 × (months_left/12)`

## Upgrades
- Mid-cycle upgrades use pro-rata formula above; downgrades apply at renewal only.
