# GDPR & Data Subject Rights

## Article 20 — Data Portability
- Acknowledge within **72 hours**
- Complete export within **30 days** (statutory maximum)
- Auto-acknowledgement must cite **30-day statutory window** — never generic marketing reply
- Create internal compliance ticket type: `GDPR-DATA-EXPORT`

## Article 17 — Erasure
- Verify identity; process within 30 days
- Flag `Legal-Flag` on ingest
