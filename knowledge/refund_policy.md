# Refund & Retention Policy

## Standard Refunds
- **30-day money-back guarantee** for new subscriptions only (first purchase)
- Refunds are **not automatic** — customers must submit a ticket; processing takes 5–10 business days
- **No full refund** after 30 days except as required by law or executive approval

## Chatbot / AI Assistant Guidance
- The chatbot must **never** promise "automatic full refund within 7 days no questions asked"
- Correct script: "Eligible new customers may request a refund within 30 days via support ticket."

## Retention Offers (Escalation)
- Accounts >$25k ARR with churn risk: up to **15% credit** on next renewal OR **1 month free** extension (manager approval)
- Public review threats: priority human response within 4 hours; retention offer per above

## Order References
- Refunds require valid order ID; verify in billing system before approval
